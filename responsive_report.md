# Responsive Design Review

We have completed a comprehensive visual and functional audit of the website across different device sizes (Mobile, Tablet, and Desktop).

## General Observations
The website maintains its premium editorial look across all viewports. Images scale correctly, and the new **Burgundy** accents remain clear and readable.

### 1. Mobile View (390px width)
- **Status**: ✅ **Passed** (after fix)
- **Observations**: 
  - The hero section adapts its height correctly.
  - Performance description text (now larger and darker) is very legible.
  - Footer aligns vertically and branding is distinct.
- **Fix Applied**: Resolved an issue where the mobile menu wouldn't automatically close when clicking internal links. 

### 2. Tablet View (768px width)
- **Status**: ✅ **Passed**
- **Observations**:
  - The recorded works slider transitions smoothly.
  - Multi-column sections (About, Guru) transition elegantly.
